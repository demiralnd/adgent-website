Privacy Policy

Effective 28 June 2026 · Last updated 28 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Adgent, a service operated by ("Adgent", "we", "us", or "our"), collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects information when you use our website and our marketing-intelligence service. A core part of Adgent is connecting, with your authorization, to your advertising and analytics platforms — including Meta, Google, TikTok, Google Analytics 4, Shopify, and Google Search Console — to read reporting data and, only on your explicit approval, carry out changes you ask us to make.

01Who we are and scope

Adgent is an AI marketing-intelligence service for performance marketers. It reads the advertising and analytics accounts you connect, tells you what is working and what is not, answers your questions, and — when you approve it — applies changes back to those accounts.

This policy covers the Adgent website and the Adgent application. It applies to two broad groups of information: (1) information about you as a user or prospective user of Adgent, and (2) the data we access from the platforms you connect on your behalf. It does not govern the platforms themselves — your use of Meta, Google, TikTok, Shopify, and related services remains subject to their own terms and privacy policies.

02Information we collect

Information you give us

  • Account & contact details — your name, work email, company, role, and similar details you provide when you request a demo, sign up, or contact us.
  • Communications — messages you send us, including support requests and the questions you ask the Adgent assistant.
  • Billing details — where you hold a paid plan, billing contact information and payment status (card processing is handled by our payment provider; we do not store full card numbers).

Information we collect automatically

  • Usage & device data — log data such as IP address, browser type, pages viewed, and actions taken, used to operate, secure, and improve the service.
  • Cookies & similar technologies — used for sign-in, security, preferences, and basic analytics. You can control non-essential cookies through your browser settings.

Connection credentials

When you connect a platform, we receive and store authorization tokens (for example, OAuth access and refresh tokens) that let Adgent access that account on your behalf. We never ask for, collect, or store your platform passwords.

03Connected platform data

With your authorization, Adgent accesses data from the platforms you connect. The data we read depends on which platforms you connect and the permissions you grant, and may include:

  • Advertising platforms (Meta, Google, TikTok) — account, campaign, ad set, and ad structure; budgets, bids, and settings; performance and delivery metrics (such as spend, impressions, clicks, conversions, ROAS, CPA, CPM, placements, and audiences). Where you grant execution access, we can apply changes you approve, such as adjusting budgets, pausing or editing campaigns, or building new campaigns.
  • Analytics & commerce sources (Google Analytics 4, Shopify, Google Search Console) — aggregated reporting data such as sessions, traffic and conversion metrics, store revenue and order metrics, and search performance, used to give your advertising data business context.

We request only the access needed to provide the features you use. We do not request access to data we do not need, and we do not "future-proof" by requesting access to data for features that do not yet exist.

Read-only by default. Adgent operates in read-only mode unless you grant execution access, and even then no change is made to your accounts until you explicitly approve it. You remain in control of every change.

04How we use information

We use information solely to provide, secure, and improve the user-facing features of Adgent. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Analyze your connected accounts and produce briefs, health scores, anomaly alerts, and reports;
  • Answer your questions about your accounts and generate the reports and audits you request;
  • Prepare recommended changes and, on your explicit approval, apply them to your connected accounts;
  • Provide support, communicate with you about the service, and manage billing;
  • Maintain the security, integrity, and reliability of the service, and detect and prevent fraud or abuse;
  • Comply with legal obligations.

Our use of data accessed through platform APIs is limited to the practices described in this policy. If we ever want to use that data in a new way, we will update this policy and obtain your consent before doing so.

05Limited use & AI commitments

We hold ourselves to strict limits on what we do with the data we access on your behalf. We do not:

  • Sell or transfer your data to third parties such as advertising platforms, data brokers, or information resellers;
  • Use your data for serving advertising, including retargeting, personalized, or interest-based advertising;
  • Use your data to determine credit-worthiness or for lending purposes;
  • Allow humans to read your data, except with your affirmative consent (for example, for support you request), where necessary for security or to investigate abuse, to comply with law, or where the data has been aggregated/de-identified and is used for internal operations;
  • Use your Connected Platform Data to train generalized or non-personalized artificial-intelligence or machine-learning models.

Adgent uses AI to analyze and reason over your data in order to deliver the service back to you. Data is processed transiently to generate your briefs, answers, and reports; it is not used to train foundation models or models for the benefit of any other customer.

Adgent's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs to any other app will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

06Legal bases

Where the GDPR or similar laws apply, we process personal data on the following bases: performance of a contract (to provide the service you sign up for); legitimate interests (to secure, maintain, and improve the service, where not overridden by your rights); consent (for example, when you authorize a platform connection or opt in to non-essential cookies); and legal obligation (to comply with applicable law). You may withdraw consent at any time.

07How we share information

We do not sell your data. We share it only as needed to run the service:

  • Service providers (sub-processors) — trusted vendors that help us host, operate, secure, and support Adgent, including cloud infrastructure providers and the AI model provider that processes data to generate your results. They may only process data on our instructions and to provide their service to us, and are contractually required to protect it and delete it when no longer needed.
  • The platforms you connect — when you approve a change, we send the corresponding instruction to that platform's API to carry it out.
  • Legal & safety — where required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Adgent, our users, or the public.
  • Business transfers — in connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets, subject to the protections of this policy and with notice to affected users.

08Data retention

We keep information only as long as needed to provide the service and for legitimate legal, security, and operational purposes. Connected Platform Data is retained while your connection is active and your account is open, and is deleted (or de-identified) when it is no longer necessary to provide the service, when you disconnect a platform, or when you delete your account — subject to limited retention required by law. You can request deletion at any time as described below.

09Your rights & data deletion

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to the processing of your personal data, to data portability, and to withdraw consent. We give every user an easily accessible way to exercise these rights:

  • Disconnect at any time — you can revoke Adgent's access to any connected platform at any time from your Adgent settings or from that platform's app/permission settings. Revoking access stops further data access immediately.
  • Request correction or deletion — email osman@adgent.app and we will action your request without undue delay.
  • Delete your account — deleting your account deletes the associated user and Connected Platform Data, except where we are required to retain limited records by law.

We will not charge you to exercise these rights, and we will not discriminate against you for doing so. If you are in the EEA or UK and have an unresolved concern, you may contact your local data protection authority.

10Security

We take reasonable and appropriate measures to protect data against unauthorized access, use, alteration, loss, or disclosure. These include encryption of data in transit and at rest, access controls and least-privilege practices, protection of access tokens and application secrets, and monitoring. We provide a clearly marked way to report security concerns, and we begin remediation and notify affected parties as required by law if an incident occurs. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to protect your data and continually improve our safeguards.

11International transfers

We may process and store information in countries other than your own, including where our service providers operate. Where personal data is transferred across borders, we rely on appropriate safeguards (such as standard contractual clauses) as required by applicable law.

12Children

Adgent is a business tool intended for use by adults aged 18 and over. It is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

13Platform-specific disclosures

Google

Adgent's use of information received from Google APIs (including Google Ads, Google Analytics 4, and Google Search Console) adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. We do not use Google user data for advertising, do not sell it, and do not use it to train generalized AI/ML models.

Meta

Data accessed through Meta's APIs is processed in accordance with this policy and with applicable Meta terms and developer policies. You can ask us to delete data accessed via Meta at any time using the contact details below, and you may revoke Adgent's access from your Meta account settings.

TikTok

When you connect TikTok, Adgent uses TikTok's developer tools to read advertising and reporting data and, where authorized, to carry out the changes you approve. Your use of TikTok remains governed by the TikTok Developer Terms and the applicable TikTok Privacy Policy. You can disconnect TikTok at any time to stop further access.

Shopify

Where you connect Shopify, Adgent reads store reporting data (such as revenue and order metrics) to give your advertising performance commercial context. We do not access more than is needed for that purpose.

14Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above and, for material changes, provide additional notice. If a change affects how we use data accessed through platform APIs, we will obtain your consent before applying the change to that data. Your continued use of Adgent after an update means you accept the revised policy.

Contact us

Questions about this policy or your data? Email osman@adgent.app.
