About

We're building the analyst, not the dashboard.

Performance marketers drown in data and starve for judgment. Adgent exists to flip that — to do the thinking, and hand back a verdict you can act on before your coffee's cold.

The market has enough tools that show you numbers. It has almost nothing that tells you what they mean.

Every performance team runs the same ritual. Open Ads Manager. Scan the campaigns. Try to remember what was normal last week. Hunt for the one thing that's quietly bleeding money. It's skilled work, done by hand, every single morning.

We think that work should be done for you — not with another chart, but with a conclusion. Adgent reads your Meta, Google and TikTok accounts as one, overnight — pulling in GA4, Shopify and Search Console for context — and writes the brief a senior analyst would leave on your desk: what changed, why it matters, and what to do about it. Ranked by impact. Evidence attached.

Then it goes one step further than a report can: when you're ready, you apply the fix — or build the next campaign — from the same chat. Read-only by default, always on your approval. Adgent decides; you confirm.

That's the whole product philosophy. Lead with the verdict. Show the evidence underneath. Act in place when asked. Say nothing you didn't need to hear — and never quote a number we can't stand behind.

How we build

Three principles, held quietly.

01 · CONSIDERED

Every number earns its place

If it doesn't change a decision, it doesn't make the brief. Each figure carries a unit and a reason for being there.

02 · CONFIDENT

The conclusion comes first

We did the analysis, so we lead with it. The evidence sits underneath for when you want to check our work.

03 · QUIET

Calm beats loud

No alarms, no badges screaming for attention. Severity means severity. The interface gets out of your way.
