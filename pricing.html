Questions

Pricing, plainly.

Do all plans really include every channel?
Yes. Meta, Google and TikTok are included on every tier — there's no "channel add-on". The difference between plans is execution and scale, not which channels you can see.
How does it pay for itself?
In our audits, a single account's first pass typically surfaces more recoverable spend than a month of Adgent costs. One real audit found roughly 31,400 TL/month on one account — about 3× the Operate subscription, before counting saved analyst hours.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Monthly plans cancel anytime, no lock-in. Annual plans get two months free in exchange for the commitment.
What counts as an ad account?
One connected Meta, Google or TikTok ad account equals one. A brand running multiple channels uses one slot per account; agencies on Scale connect as many as they manage.
What data does it read beyond ads?
Alongside Meta, Google and TikTok, Adgent reads GA4, Shopify and Search Console — so reports reflect real revenue and on-site behavior, not just platform-reported numbers.
