Questions

Plainly.

Do all plans really include every channel?
Yes. Meta, Google and TikTok are included on every tier — there's no "channel add-on". The difference between plans is execution and scale, not which channels you can see.
How is it priced?
Pricing depends on how many ad accounts you connect, how many seats you need, and whether you want execution as well as intelligence. Tell us what you're running and we'll walk you through the options.
What counts as an ad account?
One connected Meta, Google or TikTok ad account equals one. A brand running multiple channels uses one slot per account; agencies connect as many as they manage.
What data does it read beyond ads?
Alongside Meta, Google and TikTok, Adgent reads GA4, Shopify and Search Console — so reports reflect real revenue and on-site behavior, not just platform-reported numbers.
Does Adgent change my campaigns on its own?
No. Access is read-only by default. When a fix or a campaign is ready, Adgent prepares it and waits — you apply it from chat. The control is always yours; nothing is automatic.
