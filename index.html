Trust Gate

The only tool that tells you when the number is lying.

Most platforms report ROAS even when it can't be measured. Adgent doesn't. If a campaign optimizes toward a value-less event, or a metric is still inside the attribution window, we flag it — and refuse to manufacture false confidence. Honest beats impressive.

Questions

Good questions, answered plainly.

Does Adgent change my campaigns on its own?
No. Access is read-only by default. When a fix or a campaign is ready, Adgent prepares it and waits — you apply it from chat with one message. The control is always yours; nothing is automatic.
Which ad platforms does it cover?
Meta, Google and TikTok Ads, read and reasoned about as one account — and it pulls in GA4, Shopify and Search Console so reports reflect real revenue, not just platform-reported numbers.
What can I actually do from chat?
Ask about any ad, campaign, or account; run placement and demographic audits; generate custom reports; apply prepared fixes; and build cross-channel campaigns — all by message, all on your approval.
What is the Trust Gate?
It's how Adgent stays honest about numbers. If a campaign optimizes toward a value-less event, or a metric is still inside Meta's 7-day attribution window, we flag it rather than report a confident figure. We'd rather tell you a number can't be trusted than pretend it can.
Will it really pay for itself?
In our audits, a single account's first pass typically surfaces meaningful recoverable spend — before counting the analyst hours saved. Talk to sales and we'll show you what Adgent finds in your account.
Who is Adgent for?
Agencies managing many accounts, ecommerce brands running their own spend, in-house growth teams, and independent performance marketers — anyone who'd rather talk to an analyst than decode a dashboard.
